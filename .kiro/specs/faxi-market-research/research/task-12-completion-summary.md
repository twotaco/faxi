## B2C Subscription Revenue Model

### Pricing Structure

**Basic Tier: ¥980/month**
- Target: 70% of subscribers
- Features: Unlimited fax processing, email bridge, basic shopping, limited AI chat
- Rationale: Affordable on pension income, below ¥1,000 psychological price point

**Premium Tier: ¥1,980/month**
- Target: 30% of subscribers
- Features: All Basic + priority processing, advanced shopping, unlimited AI chat, 24/7 support
- Rationale: 2x Basic price captures premium value, justifies unlimited AI chat

### Key Assumptions

| Metric | Value | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| ARPU | ¥1,480/month | Weighted average (70% Basic, 30% Premium) |
| Conversion rate | 25% | SaaS industry benchmarks |
| Churn rate (Year 1) | 5% | Elderly service benchmarks |
| Churn rate (Year 3) | 3% | Improved retention |
| LTV (Year 3) | ¥37,000 | ¥1,480 × 25 months |
| CAC | ¥15,000 | Family referrals + partnerships + marketing |
| LTV:CAC ratio | 2.47:1 | Acceptable for B2C SaaS |

## Key Insights

### Strengths

1. **Diversified Revenue Streams**: B2C subscriptions provide stable base, B2B partnerships provide scalable upside
2. **Strong Unit Economics**: 80% gross margin, 2.47:1 LTV:CAC ratio, 12.7 month payback period
3. **Conservative Growth**: 0.5% → 3.0% SAM penetration accounts for elderly adoption barriers
4. **Healthy Financial Metrics**: All key metrics within or approaching industry benchmarks
5. **Scalable Model**: Both B2C and B2B channels scale proportionally

### Risks

1. **LTV:CAC Below Ideal**: 2.47:1 is acceptable but below 3:1 industry benchmark
2. **User Adoption Uncertainty**: Elderly adoption barriers may slow growth
3. **Churn Risk**: 3-5% monthly churn requires strong retention efforts
4. **Competitive Pressure**: Potential competitors may enter market by Year 3
5. **Partner Acquisition**: B2B sales cycle (3-6 months) may slow partnership growth

### Recommendations

1. **Focus on Retention**: Improve LTV:CAC ratio through churn reduction (target 2% by Year 5)
2. **Optimize CAC**: Increase referral percentage to lower acquisition costs
3. **Drive Premium Adoption**: Increase Premium tier adoption to boost ARPU
4. **Prioritize High-Value Partnerships**: Focus on e-commerce and healthcare (highest revenue per partner)
5. **Validate Assumptions**: Prove unit economics in Year 1 before scaling
