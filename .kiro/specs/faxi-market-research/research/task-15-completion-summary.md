## Key Findings

### Channel Strategy Insights

**Elder-Offline-Fax-Users**:
- Traditional, offline channels dominate
- Community-based approaches show highest conversion (1.5-6%)
- Trust and familiarity are critical success factors
- Direct mail and community partnerships offer best ROI
- Healthcare partnerships provide high-trust environment

**Online-Families**:
- Digital channels excel with this tech-savvy demographic
- Content marketing and SEO provide long-term value
- Search intent is high (actively seeking solutions)
- Social proof and testimonials drive conversion
- Multi-channel attribution important

**Business Partners**:
- Relationship-driven channels critical
- Long sales cycles (3-12 months) require patience
- Direct sales essential for enterprise deals
- Industry events provide face-to-face opportunities
- Strategic partnerships enable scalable distribution
