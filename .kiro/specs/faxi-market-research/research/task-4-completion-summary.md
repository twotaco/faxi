## Key Research Findings

### Healthcare Providers

**Market Size**:
- 8,156 hospitals + 105,182 clinics
- 1,247 geriatric hospitals, 42,000+ home healthcare agencies
- 62.3% of hospital admissions are elderly patients
- Only 34.2% of hospitals have online appointment systems
- 12.3% elderly usage rate (vs 48.7% for under-65)

**Decision-Makers**:
- Hospital Administrator (budget authority up to ¥10M)
- IT/Systems Manager (technical decisions, up to ¥5M)
- Medical Director (clinical influence, strategic partnerships)
- Patient Services Manager (patient-facing solutions)

**Procurement**:
- Timeline: 9-19 months from problem to deployment
- Pilot programs strongly preferred
- Budget cycle: April-March fiscal year
- Approval thresholds: ¥1M (dept), ¥5M (admin), ¥10M+ (board)

**Pain Points**:
1. Elderly patient accessibility (40.5% can't use online systems)
2. No-show management (8.7% rate, ¥15-30K per missed appointment)
3. Staff efficiency (30-40% time on phone appointments)
4. Regulatory compliance (Barrier-Free Act)
5. Patient satisfaction and retention

**Revenue Potential**:
- Per-facility: ¥50-200K/month
- Transaction fees: ¥500-1,000 per appointment
- Integration: ¥2-15M one-time
- Market: 5% adoption = ¥6.6 billion/year

### E-Commerce Platforms

**Market Size**:
- ¥22.7 trillion B2C e-commerce market
- 67.3% of elderly (24.7M people) don't shop online
- ¥9.2 trillion potential market if elderly participated equally
- Top categories: Food (¥2.7T), Health (¥1.8T), Household (¥1.4T)

**Decision-Makers**:
- Chief Digital Officer (¥100M-10B budget authority)
- E-Commerce Platform Manager (¥50M-2B authority)
- Customer Experience Director (¥20M-500M authority)
- Technology/IT Director (¥30M-1B authority)

**Procurement**:
- Timeline: 10-19 months from opportunity to launch
- Pilot programs required for customer-facing solutions
- Budget cycle: April-March or January-December
- Approval thresholds: ¥10M (dept), ¥50M (BU), ¥200M+ (board)

**Pain Points**:
1. Elderly market exclusion (¥9.2T untapped)
2. High customer acquisition cost (¥15-30K per elderly customer)
3. Customer service burden (3-5x more support time)
4. Accessibility compliance pressure
5. Competitive differentiation needs

**Revenue Potential**:
- Transaction fees: 3-5% (¥240-400 per ¥8K order)
- Platform fees: ¥100-500K/month
- Integration: ¥10-30M one-time
- Market: 50 platforms × 100K transactions/month = ¥18 billion/year

### Government Services

**Market Size**:
- 1,741 municipalities + 47 prefectures + 23 national agencies
- 3.3 million government employees
- ¥1.2 trillion annual IT budget (national)
- 18.7% elderly online usage (vs 67.3% under-65)
- Digital transformation mandate: all services online by 2025

**Decision-Makers**:
- Chief Information Officer (full IT budget authority)
- Digital Transformation Director (project budgets)
- Citizen Services Director (operational budgets)
- Procurement Officer (process compliance)

**Procurement**:
- Timeline: 15-36 months from needs to launch
- Public competitive bidding required (over ¥2.5M)
- Budget cycle: April-March fiscal year
- Approval thresholds: ¥5M (dept), ¥50M (CIO), ¥200M+ (council)

**Pain Points**:
1. Digital divide (14.9M elderly excluded)
2. Accessibility compliance (Barrier-Free Act)
3. Service delivery costs (¥3-5K in-person vs ¥300-500 online)
4. Citizen satisfaction and trust
5. Digital transformation mandate vs low elderly adoption

**Revenue Potential**:
- Per-municipality: ¥200K-1M/month
- Transaction fees: ¥300-1,500 per service
- Integration: ¥5-50M one-time
- Market: 20% of municipalities = ¥2.1 billion/year

### Advertisers

**Market Size**:
- ¥6.9 trillion total advertising market
- 65+ control ¥1,200 trillion in assets (60% of household wealth)
- ¥120 trillion annual consumer spending by elderly
- 40.5% of elderly unreachable via digital advertising
- Key categories: Healthcare (¥450B), Financial (¥380B), Food (¥620B)

**Decision-Makers**:
- Chief Marketing Officer (full marketing budget)
- Media Planning Director (media budget allocation)
- Digital Marketing Manager (digital budget)
- Brand Manager (campaign budgets)

**Procurement**:
- Timeline: 4-9 months from strategy to launch
- Quarterly or campaign-specific planning
- Budget cycle: April-March or January-December
- Approval thresholds: ¥10M (brand), ¥50M (director), ¥200M+ (exec)

**Pain Points**:
1. Elderly market access (14.9M unreachable digitally)
2. High customer acquisition cost (2-3x average)
3. Attribution and measurement challenges
4. Channel fragmentation
5. Lack of personalization and targeting

**Revenue Potential**:
- CPM pricing: ¥50-200 per thousand impressions
- CPA pricing: ¥5-20K per acquired customer
- Platform fees: ¥500K-5M/month
- Market: 1% of elderly ad spend = ¥280M/year, potential 5-10% = ¥1.4-2.8B/year

## Cross-Cutting Insights

### Common Decision-Making Patterns

**Consensus-Building (根回し, Nemawashi)**:
- All partner types value consensus and stakeholder buy-in
- Multiple departments and approval layers involved
- Informal pre-meetings critical
- 6-18 month sales cycles typical

**Risk Aversion**:
- Preference for proven solutions
- Pilot programs strongly preferred
- Reference customers highly valued
- Security and compliance non-negotiable

**Relationship-Oriented**:
- Long-term partnerships over transactions
- Face-to-face meetings important
- After-sales support critical
- Vendor stability matters

**Quality and Reliability**:
- High-quality, reliable service expected
- Detailed SLAs required
- Responsive support needed
- Continuous improvement valued

### Common Integration Requirements

**Technical Standards**:
- RESTful APIs with JSON/XML
- OAuth 2.0 authentication
- TLS 1.2+ encryption
- Webhook support
- Japanese documentation
- Sandbox environments

**Security and Compliance**:
- PIPA compliance mandatory
- Industry-specific regulations
- Japan data residency preferred
- Regular security audits
- Incident response procedures
- Business continuity plans

**Support and Maintenance**:
- Japanese-language support critical
- Business hours minimum, 24/7 for critical
- Dedicated account managers
- Regular updates
- Training and documentation
- Escalation procedures

### Common Budget Patterns

**Pricing Models**:
1. Transaction-based (pay per use)
2. Subscription (monthly/annual)
3. Hybrid (base + transactions)
4. Revenue sharing (percentage)

**Budget Allocation**:
- Setup: ¥2-50M one-time
- Platform fees: ¥50K-5M/month
- Transaction fees: ¥300-5K per transaction
- Maintenance: 10-20% annually

**ROI Expectations**:
- Healthcare: 12-24 months
- E-commerce: 6-18 months
- Government: 24-36 months
- Advertising: Immediate
