# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This specification defines the requirements for conducting comprehensive market research on Faxi's target Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs): elderly offline fax users, their online families, business partners, and investors. The research will inform business strategy, positioning, messaging, and website marketing copy to effectively reach and convert each audience segment. The deliverable is a structured market research document with actionable insights that directly translate into compelling marketing narratives and strategic business decisions.

## Glossary

- **ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)**: A detailed description of the type of customer who would benefit most from Faxi's services
- **Faxi System**: An AI-powered fax-to-internet bridge service enabling offline users to access online services through fax machines
- **Elder-Offline-Fax-Users**: Japanese seniors (65+) who use fax machines but do not use internet services
- **Online-Families**: Adult children and family members of elderly fax users who have internet access and digital literacy
- **Business Partners**: Healthcare providers, e-commerce platforms, government services, and other organizations seeking to reach elderly customers
- **Investors**: Venture capital firms, angel investors, and strategic investors evaluating Faxi as an investment opportunity
- **Digital Divide**: The gap between those who have access to modern digital technology and those who do not
- **Market Segmentation**: The process of dividing a target market into distinct groups with similar characteristics
- **Value Proposition**: A clear statement of the tangible benefits a customer receives from using a product or service
- **Pain Point**: A specific problem that prospective customers experience in the marketplace
- **Buying Journey**: The process a customer goes through from awareness to purchase decision
- **Competitive Landscape**: The analysis of direct and indirect competitors in the market
- **TAM (Total Addressable Market)**: The total market demand for a product or service
- **SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)**: The segment of TAM targeted by a company's products or services
- **SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)**: The portion of SAM that can realistically be captured

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a marketing strategist, I want detailed demographic and psychographic profiles of each ICP, so that I can create targeted messaging that resonates with their specific needs and values.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN analyzing elder-offline-fax-users THEN the system SHALL document age ranges, geographic distribution, income levels, education levels, living situations, and technology adoption patterns
2. WHEN analyzing online-families THEN the system SHALL document their relationship to elderly users, their digital literacy levels, their caregiving responsibilities, and their decision-making authority
3. WHEN analyzing business partners THEN the system SHALL document organization types, decision-maker roles, budget authority, and integration requirements
4. WHEN analyzing investors THEN the system SHALL document investment thesis, portfolio focus, check sizes, and evaluation criteria
5. WHEN documenting psychographic profiles THEN the system SHALL include values, fears, aspirations, communication preferences, and trust factors for each ICP

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a product manager, I want to understand the specific pain points and unmet needs of each ICP, so that I can position Faxi as the optimal solution to their problems.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN identifying pain points for elder-offline-fax-users THEN the system SHALL document barriers to accessing online services, feelings of exclusion, independence concerns, and technology anxiety
2. WHEN identifying pain points for online-families THEN the system SHALL document caregiving burden, communication challenges, safety concerns, and guilt about elderly family members
3. WHEN identifying pain points for business partners THEN the system SHALL document customer acquisition costs, market access barriers, accessibility compliance requirements, and revenue opportunities
4. WHEN identifying pain points for investors THEN the system SHALL document market size validation, technology risk assessment, competitive moat evaluation, and exit strategy concerns
5. WHEN documenting unmet needs THEN the system SHALL prioritize needs by urgency, frequency, and willingness to pay

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a content writer, I want to understand the buying journey and decision-making process for each ICP, so that I can create content that guides them toward conversion at each stage.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN mapping the buying journey for elder-offline-fax-users THEN the system SHALL document awareness triggers, consideration factors, evaluation criteria, purchase barriers, and post-purchase validation needs
2. WHEN mapping the buying journey for online-families THEN the system SHALL document how they discover solutions for elderly relatives, how they evaluate options, who they consult, and what drives their recommendation
3. WHEN mapping the buying journey for business partners THEN the system SHALL document procurement processes, stakeholder involvement, pilot program requirements, and contract negotiation factors
4. WHEN mapping the buying journey for investors THEN the system SHALL document due diligence processes, risk assessment frameworks, portfolio fit evaluation, and investment committee requirements
5. WHEN documenting decision-making processes THEN the system SHALL identify key decision criteria, deal-breakers, and conversion triggers for each ICP

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As a business strategist, I want comprehensive competitive analysis and market positioning insights, so that I can differentiate Faxi effectively in the marketplace.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN analyzing the competitive landscape THEN the system SHALL identify direct competitors, indirect competitors, and alternative solutions for each ICP segment
2. WHEN evaluating competitors THEN the system SHALL document their value propositions, pricing models, market share, strengths, and weaknesses
3. WHEN defining market positioning THEN the system SHALL identify Faxi's unique value proposition, competitive advantages, and differentiation factors for each ICP
4. WHEN analyzing market gaps THEN the system SHALL document unmet needs, underserved segments, and white space opportunities
5. WHEN assessing competitive threats THEN the system SHALL evaluate barriers to entry, potential new entrants, and substitute products or services

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** As a financial analyst, I want detailed market sizing and revenue opportunity analysis, so that I can build realistic financial projections and business models.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN calculating TAM THEN the system SHALL use credible data sources to estimate the total number of potential customers and their aggregate spending capacity
2. WHEN calculating SAM THEN the system SHALL apply realistic constraints based on geographic focus, service capabilities, and target segment characteristics
3. WHEN calculating SOM THEN the system SHALL estimate achievable market penetration based on competitive dynamics, go-to-market strategy, and resource constraints
4. WHEN analyzing revenue opportunities THEN the system SHALL document multiple revenue streams including B2C subscriptions, B2B partnerships, data monetization, and transaction fees
5. WHEN projecting market growth THEN the system SHALL analyze demographic trends, technology adoption curves, and regulatory changes that impact market size

### Requirement 6

**User Story:** As a marketing copywriter, I want compelling value propositions and messaging frameworks for each ICP, so that I can write website copy that converts visitors into customers.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN developing value propositions for elder-offline-fax-users THEN the system SHALL emphasize dignity, independence, simplicity, and familiarity
2. WHEN developing value propositions for online-families THEN the system SHALL emphasize peace of mind, reduced caregiving burden, safety, and connection
3. WHEN developing value propositions for business partners THEN the system SHALL emphasize market access, revenue growth, customer insights, and competitive advantage
4. WHEN developing value propositions for investors THEN the system SHALL emphasize market size, technology moat, scalability, and exit potential
5. WHEN creating messaging frameworks THEN the system SHALL include headline formulas, key benefit statements, proof points, and calls-to-action for each ICP

### Requirement 7

**User Story:** As a UX designer, I want to understand the communication preferences and information consumption patterns of each ICP, so that I can design website experiences that match their expectations.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN analyzing communication preferences for elder-offline-fax-users THEN the system SHALL document preferred channels, language complexity, visual design preferences, and trust signals
2. WHEN analyzing communication preferences for online-families THEN the system SHALL document information density preferences, social proof requirements, and mobile usage patterns
3. WHEN analyzing communication preferences for business partners THEN the system SHALL document B2B buying signals, case study formats, ROI calculation preferences, and technical detail requirements
4. WHEN analyzing communication preferences for investors THEN the system SHALL document pitch deck expectations, financial metric priorities, and due diligence document requirements
5. WHEN documenting information architecture THEN the system SHALL specify content hierarchy, navigation patterns, and conversion funnel design for each ICP

### Requirement 8

**User Story:** As a data analyst, I want quantitative market data and statistical validation, so that I can support marketing claims with credible evidence.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN citing market statistics THEN the system SHALL reference credible sources including government agencies, industry associations, and academic research
2. WHEN presenting demographic data THEN the system SHALL include sample sizes, confidence intervals, and data collection methodologies where available
3. WHEN making market projections THEN the system SHALL document assumptions, sensitivity analysis, and alternative scenarios
4. WHEN comparing Faxi to alternatives THEN the system SHALL use objective metrics and avoid unsubstantiated claims
5. WHEN documenting data sources THEN the system SHALL provide full citations including publication dates, URLs, and access dates

### Requirement 9

**User Story:** As a brand strategist, I want to understand cultural context and social dynamics in Japan, so that I can ensure marketing messages are culturally appropriate and effective.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN analyzing Japanese elderly culture THEN the system SHALL document values around independence, family obligation, social harmony, and technology adoption
2. WHEN analyzing family dynamics THEN the system SHALL document intergenerational relationships, caregiving expectations, and decision-making hierarchies
3. WHEN analyzing business culture THEN the system SHALL document B2B relationship building, trust establishment, and partnership expectations in Japan
4. WHEN analyzing communication norms THEN the system SHALL document appropriate tone, formality levels, and persuasion techniques for Japanese audiences
5. WHEN documenting cultural insights THEN the system SHALL identify potential cultural barriers, taboos, and opportunities for culturally-resonant messaging

### Requirement 10

**User Story:** As a growth marketer, I want to understand customer acquisition channels and conversion optimization strategies, so that I can efficiently scale Faxi's user base.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN identifying acquisition channels for elder-offline-fax-users THEN the system SHALL evaluate traditional media, community organizations, healthcare providers, and family referrals
2. WHEN identifying acquisition channels for online-families THEN the system SHALL evaluate digital advertising, content marketing, social media, and eldercare platforms
3. WHEN identifying acquisition channels for business partners THEN the system SHALL evaluate industry conferences, trade publications, direct sales, and strategic partnerships
4. WHEN identifying acquisition channels for investors THEN the system SHALL evaluate pitch competitions, investor networks, accelerators, and warm introductions
5. WHEN analyzing channel effectiveness THEN the system SHALL estimate cost per acquisition, conversion rates, and customer lifetime value for each channel and ICP combination
